Zendaya Gives a Look Inside of Her New London Home With Tom Holland

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Getty Images for SiriusXM

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Zendaya and Tom Holland are one of the most beloved celebrity couples in the entertainment industry. The Spider-Man co-stars have recently taken their relationship to the next level by moving in together. While the couple is notoriously private about their personal lives, Zendaya recently gave fans a rare glimpse into their newly shared space.

Zendaya Shared a Glimpse of Her Home With Tom Holland

The couple resides in Holland’s $3 million London home, which he purchased in January. Although Zendaya herself owns a nearly $4 million home in Los Angeles, the couple decided to settle down close to where Holland grew up, just four miles away from his hometown.

According to reports, the couple is planning to spend nearly $311,000 on a renovation that will include a gym, a movie theater, and a man cave. While the renovation is in progress, Zendaya revealed in an interview that the couple has had to learn to work around each other’s quirks to keep the peace in their new home.

“He is really good in the kitchen, which is fortunate as I am really accident-prone,” Zendaya said. “We learned early on that only one of us could be in the kitchen at a time. We are both control freaks, so we can’t be in there together.”

Despite their privacy, fans can’t help but admire the couple and their adorable dynamic. As they settle into their new home and navigate their relationship, it’s clear that Zendaya and Holland have a lot of love and respect for each other.

Tom Holland Reportedly Spent $500K on Their Home’s High-Tech Security

Holland and Zendaya are not just a talented on-screen duo, but also a real-life couple. As their love story unfolds, fans have been keeping a close eye on the couple and their whereabouts.

According to reports, Holland spent nearly $500,000 on reinforcing the security of their England home, which includes an eight-foot steel gate to keep intruders and trespassers at bay. The couple’s decision to invest in such measures is understandable, given the paparazzi’s intense interest in their relationship.

Despite their fame and success, Holland and Zendaya have been able to keep their relationship relatively private. However, Holland’s protective nature towards Zendaya has been evident, especially when it comes to their privacy.

While their relationship may be in the public eye, Holland and Zendaya’s decision to invest in their privacy and security shows that they prioritize each other’s safety and comfort above all else. As their love story continues to unfold, fans can look forward to more glimpses into their lives, both on and off screen.
