XOMG POP! to Embark on First Ever Live Tour in 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Tommaso Boddi/Getty Images for Skechers

Reading Time: 2 minutes

XOMG POP! can’t wait to hit the road for the first time. The girl group is set to embark on tour next summer of 2024.

XOMG POP! Announces Dates for Their First Live Tour in 2024

Brooklynn Pitts, Dallas Skye Gatson, Penelope LeMieux and Tinie T will be touring in the United States next year. The JoJo and Jessalynn Siwa-formed girl group broke the announcement in an Instagram post.

“We couldn’t be more excited to travel the country together and perform in new cities this summer. We have an amazing show lined up and can’t wait to Party Like Pop Stars with our fans,” XOMG POP! said in a joint statement.

XOMG POP! has been giving hints about this project for quite some time now. In late November, the girl group posted a teaser on Instagram regarding their “secret.”

“We want to tell you our secret soooo bad!!!! but our lips are sealed!!!” they wrote.

The upcoming tour will kick off on June 6 in Minneapolis, Minn. They will then make stops in Indianapolis, Chicago, Cleveland, Boston, Philadelphia, Silver Spring, Atlanta, and Orlando. Then finally, the concert will wrap up in Dallas, Texas on June 23.

Ticket sales officially began on December 5 with the Citi presale. The general sale, on the other hand, will start on Friday, December 8.

VIP packages will also be available, which may include general admission tickets with early entry, Meet & Greet + Soundcheck Party with XOMG POP!, specially designed gift item and more.

They Recently Talk Christmas EP

Ahead of Christmas, XOMG POP! released holiday record Santa! for an early treat for their fans. This collection includes Christmas classics such as “Jingle Bells” and “Little St. Nick” arranged with an ecstatic tune written by Meghan Trainor.

“We have a lot of songs on our EP, but I think all of our favorites might be ‘Jingle Bells,'” Tinie T said. “There’s a lot of different moments in the song, there’s a breakdown, there’s a rap. There’s a really fun chorus. And that’s my favorite song.”

Reflecting on their chance to work with Trainor, XOMG POP! were obviously in awe.

Whenever we’re in the studio and we’re recording with her, she just has so much energy,” Brooklyn said. “She’ll be like, ‘Get it girl!’ after every take.”

Describing the pop star “nice and welcoming,” Tinie T shared that Trainor always had “little snacks” prepared for them. She also adorably commented that Trainor’s baby is “really cute.”
