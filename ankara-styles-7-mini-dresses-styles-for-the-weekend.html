7 mini dresses styles for the weekend

The weekend is the perfect time to go vibrant Ankara pieces in different styles that make statement crafting them as fierce as possible going all out. The Ankara (African Prints) is so versatile it can be crafted in different styles to suit any body shape.

ADVERTISEMENT

To go more dramatic, the weekend is the perfect time to rock the Ankara because of the bold prints and colours it features and they are best done alone or mixed/infused with other types of materials.

Inspiration includes styles with halter neck details, form fitting cut,  backless, low cut back, skater detail,  A-line, stud button details,  high neck, bell arms and more details to make the look stand out.

Ankara in short dress styles are sexy, chic, smart and usually without fuss. They are the go to styles where a simple style with lasting impression is desired. It resonates confidence, chic, sexy and more.

For the weekend, ten specially curated styles off Instagram are presented as inspiration. Would you rock short Ankara dresses?
