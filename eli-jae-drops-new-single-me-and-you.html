Eli Jae drops new single 'Me &amp; You'

This smooth, romantic tune artfully combines the rhythmic energy of afrobeat with the soulful vibes of R&B, resulting in a melodic love song destined to touch hearts.

"Me & You" is a heartfelt testament to love and intimacy, a song that resonates deeply with the listener. It's the perfect soundtrack for weddings, proposals, and those special moments shared with a significant other. Eli Jae's smooth vocals and deep lyrics blend seamlessly with the enchanting instrumental, creating a contemporary and timeless song that will stir your emotions.

"With 'Me & You," I set out to craft a song that not only speaks to the beauty of love and the connections that bind us together but also evokes a sense of joy and romance in everyone who hears it,' says Eli Jae. His intention is clear: create a musical experience that brings a smile to your face and warmth to your heart.

About Eli Jae

Eli Jae is an emerging artist signed to Squareball Entertainment. He is known for his unique blend of Afrobeat and R&B sounds. His music, characterised by heartfelt lyrics and captivating melodies, has garnered a growing fanbase and critical acclaim. With "Me & You," Eli Jae continues to establish himself as a versatile and passionate force in the music industry.
