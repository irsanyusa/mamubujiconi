Coco the Clown (Famous Clown)

May 17, 2024 Full Name: Nicolai Poliakoff Profession: Famous Clown Biography: Coco the Clown, originally Nikolai Poliakoff, was a renowned circus performer in the 20th century who helped define and popularize the modern image of the clown. Born in Latvia, Poliakoff began his career in entertainment as a jester in a Russian travelling troupe. He honed his skills, transitioning from a performer with many roles to a dedicated clown. Poliakoff adopted the stage name Coco the Clown and gained fame through his performances with the Bertram Mills Circus in the United Kingdom.
