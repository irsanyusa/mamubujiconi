Kate Winslet (Actress) - On This Day

Profession: Actress

Biography: The youngest person to acquire six Academy Award nominations, including the 2008 Academy Award for Best Actress which she won for her role in "The Reader".

Winslet is one of the few actresses to have won three of the four major American entertainment awards (EGOT), with her Oscar, Emmy and Grammy wins.

Born: October 5, 1975
Birthplace: Reading, England, United Kingdom
Age: 48 years old

Generation: Generation X
Chinese Zodiac: Rabbit
Star Sign: Libra
