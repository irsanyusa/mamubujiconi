26 Marvel Costumes for 2022: From She-Hulk to Kate Bishop

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, Variety may receive an affiliate commission.

It’s been a busy year for Marvel, as more and more beloved heroes make their transition from the comics to the MCU, and even more Marvel mainstays get their own stand-alone films. From “She-Hulk” and “Black Widow” to “WandaVision” and “Loki,” the franchise has gifted us all with even more iterations of our favorite superheroes in the past two years — and a plethora of Halloween inspiration to pull from.

Here are the best Marvel characters to dress up as this year:

She-Hulk

One of the best things to come out of Marvel this year is Tatania Maslany’s She-Hulk in the new Disney+ series. An attorney by day and She Hulk at night, this female Hulk is an empowering character and is everything we could want. The costume is just as good as it includes a full bodysuit that zips up the back and is designed to look like Jennifer Walters when she hulks out. It also includes a pair of fingerless gloves and white boot covers.

Pick up this green wig and/or use a ton of green hair or face paint to go full on She-Hulk.
