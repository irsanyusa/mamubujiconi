Amazon Announces $25 Holiday Flights for Prime Student Members: Heres How To Get The Deal

Prime Student x StudentUniverse offers discounted flights for students to fly home for the holidays.

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, Variety may receive an affiliate commission.

With the holidays quickly coming up upon us, students from across the country will be going back home for winter break. However, since the price of flights being outrageously high and maddening these days, Amazon has joined forces with StudentUniverse to give students some financial relief on flights home.

From now until Dec. 7, Prime Student members can book flights for just $25. Yup, you read that correctly. Just $25 for domestic and returning flights when purchased through the StudentUniverse portal.

Get Prime Student X StudentUniverse

In addition, Amazon and StudentUniverse are only discounting 1,000 flights per day, so act fast and take advantage of this limited-time deal now. There are 3,000 tickets in total across three days of savings with deals starting at 6 a.m. PST/9 a.m. EST on Dec. 5, as well as 12 a.m. PST/3 a.m. EST on Dec 6. and Dec. 7.

Not a member? Sign up for a six-month free trial to take advantage of all that Prime Student has to offer, including access to Prime Video and Prime Music; fast same-, next-, or two-day free shipping with Prime Delivery; free meal delivery from GrubHub+, access to exclusive shopping events — like Prime Day and Black Friday — and more. After the free trial is over, Prime Student goes for $7.49 per month.

Keep in mind, you have to be an active student with a valid university or college email (ending in .edu), school ID card or proof of enrollment to sign up for Prime Student.

Meanwhile, flights have to be taken between Dec. 8 and Dec. 25, while return flights have to be booked between Dec. 8 to Jan. 14, 2024 to be eligible for discounted prices.
