Steven Seagal (Actor) - On This Day

Profession: Actor

Biography: Steven Seagal began his working life as a martial arts instructor teaching Aikido.

He later moved into films starring in a number of action adventure Hollywood films. His best known role is as the Navy SEALs counter-terrorist expert Casey Ryback in the 1992 film "Under Siege".

Seagal went on to have a singing career, releasing his debut album "Songs from the Crystal Cave" in 2005.

Seagal is a strong supporter of Russian leader Vladimir Putin and was granted Russian citizenship in 2016.
