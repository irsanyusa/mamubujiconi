Mixed-ish Star Mykal-Michelle Harris Shares Her Best TikTok Tips

“Mixed-ish” actor Mykal-Michelle Harris revealed that not only is she very similar to Santamonica Johnson, her character on the “Black-ish” spinoff, but she’s also quite the TikTok star.

The 7-year-old shared some of her best TikTok tips with Variety’s Audrey Cleo Yap. “Do originals. Create your own,” she said. “Me and my sister [on camera] Arica, we’re always creating our own.”
