12 Bachelorettes That Chose Single Lives and Dont Regret It

Back in the previous century, Coco Chanel and Greta Garbo decided to be single for different reasons. And today, many famous women follow the examples of these successful ladies. Kylie Minogue, Charlize Theron, and Emilia Clarke have never been married and can hardly imagine themselves as wives.

We at Bright Side found out who among very popular and beautiful women decided not to tie the knot and have no regrets.

Charlize Theron, 46 years old

Charlize Theron has never been married and is not going to. According to her, marriage has never been something important to her. Theron doesn’t feel lonely — she gives all of her love to her 2 adopted children.

Emilia Clarke, 35 years old

Emilia Clarke, one of the most beautiful women in the world, is not married. The actress is thinking about stopping dating actors because good relationships between successful actors are very rare. Clarke only trusts herself now.

Kristin Davis, 57 years old

Unlike her character Charlotte from Sex and the City, Kristin Davis has never been married, nor has she ever dreamed of it. The actress has no time for dating because she is raising her 2 children which makes her happy.

Kylie Minogue, 53 years old

Kylie Minogue doesn’t think she will get married because it has never been her goal. In 2017, her relationship with Joshua Sasse ended. Minogue said that the engagement was an experiment for her and marriage was not something she really wanted.

Diane Keaton, 76 years old

Diane Keaton has dated legendary Hollywood men, from Al Pacino to Warren Beatty. None of her relationships ended in marriage though, and the actress doesn’t think that’s a problem. Keaton lives a happy and full life: she continues to appear in popular movies, and after she turned 50 years old, she adopted 2 children.

Oprah Winfrey, 68 years old

Oprah Winfrey found no place in her life for marriage and children, and she doesn’t regret it. She thinks that being a mother and a wife is very hard work, and all of her time is busy with her legendary show.

Lucy Liu, 53 years old

Even though Lucy Liu got engaged to Zach Helm, they never got married. Instead, Liu devoted her life to what she loves and her son, Rockwell Lloyd.

Dana Delany, 66 years old

Dana Delany never wanted to “settle down.” The Desperate Housewives star had many opportunities to get married but she loves being alone so much that only a very special person could make her change her attitude.

Winona Ryder, 50 years old

Winona Ryder has had short relationships and long romances but she’s never been married. She says that marriage is not the most important thing in life for her.

Patricia Kaas, 55 years old

Patricia Kaas says that her passion for singing takes up most of her time. According to her, she’s not an easy person to live with, and a man that appears in her life has to accept the fact that her biggest love is her fans.

Edie Falco, 58 years old

Star of The Sopranos, Edie Falco never had marriage as a priority. She says that being a good mother to her 2 adopted children is much more important than marriage and relationships.

Sheryl Crow, 60 years old

Sheryl Crow has never been married, but the singer has been engaged 3 times. At the moment, she is happy raising 2 children and doesn’t deny the fact she might get married one day. She says she has very high demands for her potential husband.
