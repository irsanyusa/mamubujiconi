What's New on Netflix in June 2024

June is the month for steamy romances on Netflix. First, Bridgerton returns June 13 with the second part of its third season, then Fifty Shades of Grey follows on June 18. And for fans of 1980s teen angst, The Breakfast Club hits the streaming site on June 1. Also available to stream June 1 is La La Land, fun for those who loved Emma Stone in her Oscar-winning role in Poor Things (2024). Crazy Rich Asians can be watched on June 6, just in time for Kevin Kwan's latest book Lies and Weddings. And comedian David Letterman has a new season of his talk show debuting on June 12. Here’s everything coming to Netflix in June 2024—and what’s leaving.
