How to Be an Audience Member For 'Canadas Got Talent' Season 3 Auditions

Photo by Jeremychanphotography/Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

As Canada’s Got Talent gears up for its third season on CityTV, the show is seeking audience members. The reality competition begins filming soon, if you want to be in the audience, continue reading!

Canada’s Got Talent is Looking for Studio Audience for Auditions

Canada’s Got Talent season three will begin filming auditions this October. While some fans may have secured their tickets by now, there are still plenty for those who may not have signed up yet.

“Be part of the studio audience for the Canada’s Got Talent auditions this October at the OLG Stage at @Fallsviewcasino, in Niagara Falls! We can’t wait to see you there! #CGT,” the caption reads.

Free tickets are first come, first serve, so be sure to secure your place sooner rather than later. CityTV did not specify the age range they would prefer for the audience.

According to the FAQ, tickets are transferable if the fan will not be able to attend. Furthermore, while there’s no specific dress code, the show encourages audiences to look the same way as they would for a live concert or play.

Amid live taping, audience members are expected to stay for the full duration of the taping. It will take approximately four hours. Other details such as time of required arrival and car parking can be found on CityTV’s official website.

The Show is Searching for Their First Ever One Million Dollar Winner

Canada’s Got Talent is still currently taking applications for their upcoming third season. Just like the previous season, the show is looking for either solo acts or group acts to grace the show.

“Show us what you got, Canada! Apply NOW for #CGT season 3 at http://Citytv.com link in bio,” the post read. “The lucky winner will receive ONE MILLION DOLLARS thanks to @Rogers!”

In season 2, Trois-Rivières dance troupe Conversion snatched the trophy. Walking away as champions, the Quebec group claimed the $150,000 grand prize. This year, the stakes are raised, as the winning prize has been increased to $1 Million dollars!

“Got Talent is the world’s greatest talent competition for acts of all ages and sets out to discover the most unique, skilled, heartwarming and showstopping performers Canada has to offer. Dancers, singers, illusionists, comedians, and every imaginable talent in between will compete before a panel of celebrity judges, a live studio audience and the entire nation. With compelling stories and mesmerizing performances, this competition is unlike any other,” the CityTV website describes CGT.
